Video Game Hangman!

Made by Vishal Madhav

Hello and welcome to my game, Video Game Hangman! The rules are very simple!  You have to guess the name of a video game by using the buttons of letters provided to you in order to guess the word for that random question.

But be careful!  You only get 10 guesses to guess the answer. If all of those 10 guesses are wrong, you will lose and game is over!

Good luck!!
